## Critical Findings

### Authentic Knowledge Extracted

#### Three Cultural Pillars (Foundation of Umwero)
1. **Imana (God)**
   - Hero na Herezo (Alpha & Omega)
   - Circle symbolism (no beginning, no end)
   - "Ibanze" - God as starting of all things

2. **In'ka (Cattle)**
   - Inyambo cows with long horns
   - Letter A = cow's head and horns
   - Letter B + A = complete cow shape
   - Sound "Baaaa" vocalization

3. **Ingoma (Throne/Kingdom)**
   - King Gihanga Ngomijana (100 thrones)
   - Throne symbol for hundreds in numbers
   - "Akami ka muntu ni umutima we" - kingdom is in the heart

#### Character Cultural Meanings
- **M (Ma)**: Womb/motherhood, umbilical cord (Umura)
- **D (Da)**: Masculinity/fatherhood, male symbol
- **J (Ja)**: Slave/laborer, bowed figure
- **MF (Mfa)**: Noble person (Imfura), supporting others
- **PF (Pfa)**: Death, circle disrupted by line
- **C (Ca/Cha)**: Serpent/pathway, wisdom
- **T & K**: Featural letters aligned with IPA
- **S & TS**: Sibilant sounds, unstoppable flow

#### Founder's Journey
- **2014-2019**: Discovery through rap songwriting
- **2017**: Nairobi visit revealed colonial writing inconsistencies
- **2019**: Mirror revelation - circle never changes
- **Measurement of 8**: Umunani (heritage/inheritance)
- **Craig Cornelius**: Helped develop virtual keyboard
